Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

At colcel.shop, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Shipping address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed exclusively by PayPal)
  • Purchase history

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process orders and payments
  • Send confirmations and updates
  • Improve user experience
  • Prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • PayPal – payment processing
  • Logistics providers – for shipping
  • Zapier – automation tool
  • Google Sheets – operational storage

We never sell or rent your data.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of use of your data by writing to privacy@colcel.shop.

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@colcel.shop.
